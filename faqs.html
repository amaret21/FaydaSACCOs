Frequently Asked Questions

Membership

To become a member of Fayda SACCO:

  1. Complete the membership application form (available online or at any branch)
  2. Provide copies of your ID document
  3. Pay the membership fee of 200 ETB
  4. Make your initial deposit (minimum 100 ETB)

Your membership will be activated within 24 hours of completing these steps.

Savings

We offer several savings products:

  • Regular Savings: Minimum 100 ETB, withdraw anytime, earns 5% interest
  • Fixed Deposit: Minimum 5,000 ETB, fixed terms (3-24 months), higher interest
  • Target Savings: Save towards specific goals with flexible contributions
  • Youth Savings: Special account for members under 25 years
