Discussion to Establish SACCOs

On Sunday, October 13, 2024, the first discussion to establish Saving and Credit Co-Operative Society (SACCOs) in Adama Town. The event was attended by various stakeholders, including government officials and community leaders. The discussion focused on the benefits of SACCOs in promoting savings and providing affordable credit to members. Participants expressed their enthusiasm for the initiative and committed to supporting its establishment.

Training Session

The comprehensive training session held on November 5, 2024, provided SACCO members with essential financial management skills. The two-day workshop covered topics including bookkeeping, loan management, and cooperative principles. Over 50 participants gained practical knowledge to effectively manage their finances and contribute to the SACCO's success.

Branch Opening

The grand opening of our new branch office on December 15, 2024, marked a significant milestone for Fayda SACCOs. The ceremony was attended by local dignitaries, members, and community representatives. The new branch will improve access to our services and support the growing membership in the region.
