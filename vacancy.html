Loan Officer

Full-time
Addis Ababa Deadline: Dec 15, 2023

Job Summary

We are looking for an experienced Loan Officer to evaluate, authorize approval or deny loan applications for individuals or businesses. You will act as liaison between customers and our SACCO and help qualified applicants acquire loans in a timely manner.

Responsibilities

  • Meet with applicants to obtain information for loan applications
  • Analyze applicants' financial status and creditworthiness
  • Determine all applicable ratios and metrics
  • Communicate with clients about loan decisions
  • Maintain and update account records

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics or related field
  • 2+ years proven experience as loan officer
  • Familiarity with computers and banking applications
  • Solid understanding of lending products
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

Full-time

We are looking for an IT Support Specialist to maintain and support our SACCO's computer systems, networks, and provide technical assistance to staff. You will diagnose and resolve technical issues and ensure our technology infrastructure runs smoothly.

Internship Program

Gain Valuable Experience with Our Internship Program

Fayda SACCO offers internship opportunities for university students in various departments including Finance, Marketing, IT, and Customer Service. Our internship program provides hands-on experience in the financial cooperative sector.

Requirements
