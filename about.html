About Fayda SACCOs

Fayda SACCOs, a member-owned financial cooperative since October 31, 2024, offers innovative services, savings, loans, and investments for community financial inclusion and development through member empowerment.

Become a Member

Our Mission

Improve socio-economic conditions of members & other people through mobilizing saving and creating access to finance using modern technology and highly motivated staffs.

Our Vision

To be a preferred Saving and Credit Cooperative Association by 2030 in Ethiopia, recognized for excellence in member service, financial sustainability, and community development.

Objective

Build a saving culture and facilitate credit with a fair interest rates and improve the socio-economic conditions of members and other people by equipping them with entrepreneurship and business development skills. And ultimately contribute to the poverty reduction effort of the country by creating employment opportunities especially for women and youth.

Our Core Values

Cooperation

We believe in working together for mutual benefit and shared success.

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices.

Member Focus

Our members are at the center of everything we do.

Innovation

We continuously improve our services to better serve our members.
